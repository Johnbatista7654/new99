The Security OS

Crypto UX and regulatory hurdles are largely behind us. The $300B and growing stablecoin boom signals the arrival of the onchain economy. The next wave of innovation from DeFi, RWAs (like tokenized stocks), and new onchain financial services is being driven by Wall Street and billions of digital dollar holders seeking yield.

But this opportunity comes with the risk of catastrophic losses from hacks, making security the next big hurdle for bringing the next decatrillion onchain.

In the first half of 2025 alone, more than $2B was stolen from protocols. Institutions wont move trillions onchain when over 3% of assets are lost annually. The threat landscape now spans far beyond smart contract exploits. Multisig compromises, scams, fraud, compliance failures, brand attacks, and relentless AI-driven threats overwhelm todays fragmented, reactive, human-operated defenses.

As the onchain economy matures, a new standard of protection must emerge, one capable of defending this digital financial infrastructure at scale.

Thats why Immunefi is launching the first Security OS for the Onchain Economy: a unified command center delivering complete, real-time security.

At the core of the OS is Immunefi AI, which is trained on the worlds largest dataset of real exploits, bug reports, and mitigations, continuously expanding through audits, onchain monitoring, formal verification, fuzzing, and bug bounties. This intelligence analyzes each customers security posture, predicting and neutralizing threats at machine speed.

With Immunefi, security becomes predictive, integrated, and enterprise-ready, enabling capital to move onchain with confidence and scale.

The Security OS pre-launch alpha already secures over $81 billion in assets from harm. It has 120+ early access members from the largest projects in Web3, including Chainlink, Optimism, Avalanche, ENS, Lido, Polygon, Aave, The Graph, Sei, Morpho, and many more. From transactions to high-value treasuries, every movement of value in Web3 will be protected by the Security OS, stopping hacks before they happen. Our goal is to secure the entire Web3 TVL, projected to reach trillions.

Token Utility

IMU will establish an economic layer designed to accelerate the security flywheel by aligning incentives across the three forces that work to make Web3 safe:

  1. Protocols seeking end-to-end protection
  2. Security technologists & researchers building and delivering it
  3. Protocol users and community members who benefit from safer financial products

Inspired by Vitaliks call to do well by doing good, IMU is designed to reward contributors who help secure the onchain economy, and aims to create a shared mission of every contributor participating in the upside.

CREDITS PROGRAM

Protocols use the Immunefi token to access deeper platform tools, unlock Security OS discounts, and earn IMU credits as they improve their defenses. Every engagement generates real-world security data that trains Immunefi AI. The more projects onboard, the smarter the system gets. By staking IMU, protocols gain premium features and greater protection, which fuels the OS that safeguards the entire onchain economy.

BOOST PROGRAM

Immunefis 60,000-strong network of elite security researchers earns IMU for producing high-value threat datazero-days, audits, and exploit reportsthat trains Immunefi AI. Staking IMU unlocks higher reward tiers and exclusive opportunities. The more researchers contribute, the stronger the AI becomes, creating a virtuous loop that protects trillions in onchain assets.

PATRONS PROGRAM

The Patrons Program lets community members stake IMU to back either security researchers or protocols they believe in. By staking behind researchers, supporters help them reach higher contribution tiers and share in their rewards. The greater the researchers impact and the larger the stake, the higher the shared IMU returns. By staking behind protocols, communities directly strengthen a projects security initiatives and earn rewards for supporting a safer ecosystem. In both cases, staking unlocks onchain rewards for advancing the shared goal of a more secure web3 economy.


The IMU genesis supply of 10,000,000,000 is distributed across four main allocation pools, each serving a strategic purpose in the ecosystem,

ECOSYSTEM & COMMUNITY 47.5%

Nearly half of the tokens support ecosystem growth and community rewards. This pool funds pre-listing sales, user incentives (e.g. bug bounty bonuses, staking rewards), partnerships, airdrops, marketing, and liquidity. The large allocation ensures the token directly drives adoption and engagement.

EARLY BACKERS 16%

Early backers receive 16% for supporting the projects mission and growth. Tokens unlock over three years to create long-term alignment.

TEAM & CORE CONTRIBUTORS 26.5%

Core contributors at Immunefi receive 26.5%, locked over 36 months to ensure continued development and alignment with long-term value creation. The team must earn their tokens over time, which incentivizes continuous development and aligns the builders with long-term value creation.

RESERVE 10%

A small portion is held in reserve by the treasury for future needs and unforeseen opportunities to accelerate platform growth. This 10% acts as a safety buffer and growth fund. It can be deployed for things like further ecosystem grants or emergency expenses as the project evolves.
