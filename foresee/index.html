Project Details

1. Overview

Native to Seeker, powered by Jupiter, and anchored by Polymarket, Foresee is the definitive mobile-based prediction layer for the Solana ecosystem. 

Foresee replaces the friction of search-based markets with the fluid discovery of a social feed and embeds real-time signals directly into the conversation. 

Foresee achieves something profoundly meaningful by building the prediction layer directly atop a social interface, right on your mobile device, which transforms forecasting from a rigid financial chore into a natural extension of your daily routine.

Like a grand reveal, Foresee, as the social prediction-market application, lets you post, share, uncover high-conviction opportunities and trade predictions with your friends in real time.

In just 4 weeks since launch, Foresee has secured the following milestones

  • Secured 1st place at the Indiefun Hackathon

  • Onboarded 1,850+ active Solana Seeker users

  • Facilitated over 3,700 high-conviction predictions

  • Captured $140,000 in total volume during the beta phase

Foresee’s launch as a Seeker-native application was amplified by key Solana pioneers and infrastructure partners, including Toly, Solana Mobile, Jupiter Prediction, and Privy. The momentum was further solidified through institutional integration with Polymarket and dedicated endorsement from Solana’s most prestigious builder communities, MonkeDAO and Superteam.

Core Features of Foresee Beta:

  • Specialized market discovery: Effortlessly track top-volume sectors and 24-hour volatility.

  • Live social feed: A dynamic live feed that lets you see what friends and followers predict in real time

  • Global leaderboards: Track the elite across daily, weekly, and all-time performance rankings.

  • Ultimate Market Aggregator: Aggregate the deep liquidity of the world’s largest prediction markets directly to your social feed and natively to your phone, powered by Jupiter’s elite routing and Polymarket’s institutional infrastructure

  • Permissionless access: Enjoy a non-custodial, zero KYC experience with the seamless security of Privy.
