    CCA is an onchain auction protocol for fair, transparent liquidity bootstrapping. Projects commit a portion of supply, set parameters, and the auction handles distribution, price discovery, and liquidity creation automatically.

    Each bid is spread across all auction blocks. At the end of every block, demand is aggregated against the scheduled token supply, and a clearing price is set. As demand (bids) accumulate, the clearing price increases and sets a new floor for future auction blocks. If demand is low, tokens remain unfilled at the floor price. Over time, this process uncovers the market price.

    Our CCA enables market-driven token distribution, enables open and transparent participation, and provides builders with an onchain alternative to centralized exchange liquidity.

    Bids are distributed across the entire auction duration and all participants pay the same clearing price. Transaction speed provides no advantage. Only your bid price and commitment matters.
