What are you building?

Crypto has a trust problem. Airdrops get farmed and dumped - 90% of tokens sold within a week. Presales get sniped by bots. Protocols can't tell real users from sophisticated farmers. The 99% who participate in good faith get treated the same as the 1% gaming the system.

FairScale fixes this.

We're building a universal reputation score - FairScore - that measures on-chain behavior, social signals, and community standing. Privacy-preserving by design. Real-time. Integration-ready.

For users: FairScore is your on-chain resume. Everything you've done - holding tokens, participating in governance, building community rep - now works for you. High scores unlock priority presale access, better loan terms, and undiluted airdrop allocations.

For protocols: Finally distinguish genuine community members from farming wallets. Use FairScore to weight allocations, gate presales, or tier lending terms. Stop wasting budget on users who dump in 72 hours.

Traction: Live pilots X Bank, Kast, Star.fun, Streamflow and more. 3000+ users, 7.5m transactions and 50k social and sentiment signals analysed for model validation.

The flywheel: More users build FairScores better data, smarter algorithm more valuable API for protocols more protocols integrate more opportunities unlock for high-FairScore users repeat.

What does your token do?

The FAIR token is an ownership coin, and is used in the futarchy governance model. It means that owning the token = owning the IP, treasury.

The tokens distribution was determined with our values in mind, aiming for incentive alignment and a Fair valuation:

  • 60% of the total supply will be sold during the ICO

  • 21% is reserved as the teams incentive package, released on market cap milestones, measured by 30-day TWAP. The teams starting allocation is 0%, with first tranche of 2.5% of the total supply being unlocked at $10m market cap. The last tranche is unlocked at $320m market cap.

  • 12% of the total supply will be locked as protocol owned AMM liquidity

  • 5% of the total supply is assigned to futarchy governed treasury

  • 2% will be rewarded to $STAR stakers following 30 day linear vesting

  • 1% will be distributed to ICO participants based on their FairScore

A great showcase of the Futarchy Governance dynamic is the handling of the 5% token Treasury. It isnt controlled by the team or a multisig. Its governed by token holders through decision markets. Want to fund a partnership? Propose it. The market decides if its likely to create value.

The governance is facilitated by Combinator. Initially the proposals can be put forward by the team, or token holders of 3% or more of total supply, who held it for more than 30 days (measured by TWAP). This measure ensures long term outlook in decision making and incentive alignment.
